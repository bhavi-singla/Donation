What Do Our Dogs Eat?

Our dogs are fed human-grade food – if it’s not safe for humans, it’s definitely not safe for our dogs. We feed our dogs 200kg of rice and 100kg of chicken in one meal and we make 2 meals a day. We use 10 tonnes of dry food a month that includes rice, chicken, potatoes, curd and kibble. Additionally, to ensure our dogs get adequate nutrition, we add iron + multivitamin + liver supplements to the food.
